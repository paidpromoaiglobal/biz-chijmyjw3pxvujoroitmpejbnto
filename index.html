Our Legacy

Ghoonghat

Ghoonghat is a beloved ethnic wear destination nestled in the heart of Sowcarpet, George Town, Chennai — one of the city's most vibrant shopping hubs. Specialising in ghagra cholis, lehengas, and a wide range of traditional Indian clothing, Ghoonghat has earned the trust of customers with its beautiful designs, quality fabrics, and budget-friendly pricing. With a rating of 4.6 out of 5, the store stands as a go-to choice for women seeking stunning outfits for weddings, festivals, and every special occasion.

Located on iconic Mint Street near Chandra Prabhu Jain Naya Mandir and adjacent to Amrit Hospital, Ghoonghat is easy to find and a pleasure to visit. The team prides itself on warm, customer-friendly service that makes every shopper feel welcome. Whether you are searching for the perfect bridal ensemble or a festive ethnic outfit, Ghoonghat offers an extensive collection that caters to varied tastes and budgets — all under one roof.
